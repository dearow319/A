> Operation Cosmo
---
Objective:

Eliminate Yuri Gagarin due to the failure of the experiments and the loss of utility of the suspected alien shapeshifter.

Plan Details:

	Preparation:
	- Increase surveillance on Gagarin.
	- Prepare backup pilot Vladimir Seryogin.
	- Timeline: Operation over 2-3 years.

	Execution:
	- Stage an aviation accident involving Gagarin and Seryogin.
	- Use a remote-controlled aircraft to simulate the crash.

	Containment:
	- Isolate Gagarin post-accident.
	- Transport to secure facility for final disposal.

	Post-Operation:
	- Confirm elimination.
	- Gather and document findings.
	- Dismiss experiments.

- V
